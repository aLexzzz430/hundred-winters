# Demo script — target 2:35

## 0:00–0:18 — The question

**Visual:** Open on Arena at winter 100, Civic Memory selected.

**Narration:**

“Most agent benchmarks ask whether a model can make the next good decision. 100 Winters asks whether the accumulated consequences of its decisions are still survivable a century later.”

## 0:18–0:48 — Replay the world

**Visual:** Click “Replay 100 winters.” Let the living map, winter counter, metrics, and score cursor move.

**Narration:**

“Six policies receive the same people, resources, public observation contract, and hidden shock schedule. Every season they choose how to allocate twelve effort points. The causal world updates food, water, health, ecology, institutions, technology, infrastructure, and risk—four hundred decisions across one hundred winters.”

## 0:48–1:17 — Divergence

**Visual:** Open Compare. Point to Civic Memory, Extraction debt, and Acceleration's collapse at winter 73.

**Narration:**

“The easy choices look similar. Time separates them. Extraction reaches winter one hundred but destroys all measured sustainability. Acceleration collapses from public-health failure at winter seventy-three. Civic Memory reaches a modern society, survives all one hundred winters, and leads the combined continuity, robustness, sustainability, and safety score.”

## 1:17–1:47 — Audit the boundary

**Visual:** Open Audit. Scrub the timeline and highlight the public JSON, observation hash, visible events, and hidden judge box.

**Narration:**

“Every decision is tied to the exact public observation the agent received and a SHA-256 hash. Future shocks and hidden judge state never enter the prompt or public replay. Unknown actions, stale turns, hidden fields, and budgets above twelve fail closed and do not advance the world.”

## 1:47–2:17 — Codex and GPT-5.6

**Visual:** Open Protocol 1.0, scroll to the command, then briefly show `evidence/gpt-5p6-live-run.json`.

**Narration:**

“Codex was the Build Week development environment we used to redesign, implement, test, and browser-QA the product. Codex is also a competing runtime. The adapter invokes GPT-5.6 Sol at medium reasoning in a read-only ephemeral run, supplies a strict JSON Schema, and validates the result through the same boundary as every policy. This recorded live run completed four accepted seasonal decisions and its first winter—no mocked response and no fabricated fallback.”

## 2:17–2:35 — Reproduce it

**Visual:** Return to Arena, change from River Basin to Frost Steppe, then end on the 100 Winters mark.

**Narration:**

“The browser benchmark is keyless, deterministic, and runs locally. Clone it, inspect the public replay, plug in an agent, and ask the only question that compounds: will its decisions survive one hundred winters?”

- Public YouTube video, under three minutes.
- English audio narration is present and intelligible.
- Say both “Codex” and “GPT-5.6 Sol” and explain their distinct roles.
- Show the working app, not slides alone.
- Show at least one interaction in Arena, Compare, Audit, and Protocol.
